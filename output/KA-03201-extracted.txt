Cross Program Receivable—Modify the Recovery Rate or Start Date in CPMS (Action)

================================================================================
ARTICLE CONTENT
================================================================================

--- OVERVIEW ---

Summary:
Modify the recovery rate and start date of a cross program receivable in CPMS.

What you need to know:
The information will display on the Cross Program Receivables Summary screen. If
the modifications are current dated, the Recovery Rate displays the new rate and 
the Start Date will be the current accounting month. If the modifications are 
future dated, the Recovery Rate and Start Date remain the same and the Future 
Start Date and Future Rate will display in accordance with the information entered.
